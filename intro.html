Introduction of Movie Database

Welcome to my movie database. Here in this database you can search for movies, actors, director, or even writers.

This movie database has seven entities. Their names are as follows; Movie, Director, Writer, Actor, Genre, Award and Person. There are seven relationship and an ISA hierarchie to connect those entities. Lets explain some of these relationships. A movie has to have one or more writer, director, actor and genre. Writers, directors and actors, are(IS-A) person. Movie, and people who worked in movie could be awarded.
